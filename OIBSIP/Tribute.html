The King Khan: Mini Biography

Shah Rukh Khan was born on November 02, 1965 in New Delhi to Meer Taj Mohammed Khan & Lateef Fatima Khan. He also has an elder sister, Shehnaz Lalarukh. He spent his early days in Bangalore with his grandparents. His father used to run a restaurant in Delhi. His father died in 1981 and mother in 1991. And after the death of their parents, his sister fell into a depressed state. So, Khan along with his sister, shifted to Mumbai where Khan started his film career. He is an Indian actor, film producer, entrepreneur and television personality. He married Gauri Chhibber on October 25, 1991. They have three children, son Aryan (b. 1997), AbRam (b. 2013) and daughter Suhana (b. 2000). Khan started out his career by appearing in several television serials during 1988-1990. He made his film debut with the commercially successful Deewana (1992). He began to achieve success at the box office from his villainous roles in films such as Darr (1993), Baazigar (1993), and Anjaam (1994). During his years in the Bollywood film industry, he won eight Filmfare best actor awards, which is highest by any actor and had significant box-office success. Some of his films include Kuch Kuch Hota Hai (1998), Om Shanti Om (2007), and Chak De! India (2007).

Shah Rukh Khan, often referred to as the "King of Bollywood," is renowned for his exceptional work ethic and unwavering dedication to his craft. Known for his charismatic on-screen presence and versatility as an actor, Khan has built an illustrious career that spans over three decades. Throughout his career, Shah Rukh Khan has consistently displayed a relentless work ethic, often going above and beyond to perfect his roles. He is known to invest considerable time and effort into understanding the nuances of his characters, immersing himself in their world to deliver authentic performances.

Khan's commitment to his work is evident in his punctuality and professionalism on set. He is known for his disciplined approach, ensuring that he is fully prepared for each scene. His dedication is further exemplified by his willingness to push his physical and emotional boundaries to bring his characters to life. Beyond his acting prowess, Shah Rukh Khan's work ethic is also reflected in his entrepreneurial ventures. He has successfully established his own production company, Red Chillies Entertainment, and has taken on the role of a producer for several films. Khan's entrepreneurial spirit and business acumen showcase his hardworking nature and his desire to constantly evolve and explore new avenues.
Shah Rukh Khan's unparalleled success in the entertainment industry is a testament to his tireless efforts and relentless pursuit of excellence. His commitment to his craft, combined with his natural talent, has cemented his status as one of the most influential and hardworking personalities in the world of entertainment.
